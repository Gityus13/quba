üöó How to Get to Quba

By Car

2‚Äì2.5 hour drive from Baku via the Guba highway. The road is well-maintained with beautiful scenery.

Baku International Bus Terminal has daily buses to Quba. Tickets cost about 5-7 AZN (3-4 USD).

Many travel agencies in Baku offer day or weekend trips to Quba, often combined with other northern destinations.
