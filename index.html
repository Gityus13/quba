About Quba

A picturesque region in northeastern Azerbaijan known for its stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage

The Heart of Azerbaijan's Mountain Culture

Quba is a breathtaking region located in the northeastern part of Azerbaijan, nestled between the majestic Caucasus Mountains and the Caspian Sea. This area is renowned for its pristine nature, traditional villages, and the warm hospitality of its people.

The region boasts a unique microclimate that creates perfect conditions for growing delicious fruits, particularly apples and pears that Quba is famous for throughout Azerbaijan.

Must-See Attractions

Discover the wonders that make Quba a unique destination

Khinalig Village

One of the highest and most ancient villages in the Caucasus, offering breathtaking views and unique cultural traditions.

Tenghi Canyon

Often called "Azerbaijan's Grand Canyon," this natural wonder features dramatic rock formations and stunning vistas.

Apple Orchards

Visit the famous orchards of Quba, where you can pick fresh apples and learn about traditional farming methods.
